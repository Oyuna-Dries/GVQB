About

About Us.

Gentlemen from all corners of Bristol and further a field flock to our unique men’s salon for the pampering and high level of care they know they will receive. Customers are confident that they can count on our discretion and a professional service. They know they will leave our salon feeling and looking their best. We aim to be your home from home, where you always feel special and welcome within our relaxed, unhurried surroundings, all designed to ensure your total relaxation.
